AL Grill Liverpool

AL Noor Grill is quite simply a little piece of Yemen that sits on the chic and quirky Bold Street in Liverpool. This most beautiful and lovingly crafted restaurant is, from start to finish an experience and flavour of the Mediterranean.

Our food menu is created with magic and mystery at every turn and the menu is a treasure hunt of culinary tastes, vision and feel.
